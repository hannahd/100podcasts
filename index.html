Challenge to try two new podcasts per week in 2019.

I love podcasts. I listen to them almost every day. But I have a confession. I pretty much stick to the same few favorites. Meanwhile, all the subscriptions with intriguing descriptions and suggestions from friends languish unlistened to at the bottom of my list. This year, I'm changing that.

Want to join me & start your own challenge? Message me on Twitter or fork this repo on GitHub.
